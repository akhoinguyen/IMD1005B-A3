Hello and welcome :)

I've been wanting to go wild and experimental with a personal website and I thought what better way to implement that than for this assignment.

This aesthetic pretty much speaks to the creative and fun side of me that I don't get to do often. I feel like as creatives you can get clouded by your work and diluted by others inputs and sometimes forget to have fun. No AODA this time around, no non designers yapping about how the font is less than 17.59823pt and how the red is not "warm" and "friendly" enough -_-.

If you grade this site at night in an all dark room, I'm sorry ... but regardless enjoy this bombardment of colours and interesting design.
